Importance of e-waste Recycling

E-waste is growing, and with that surge comes the need for effective electronics Recycling programs. According to a January 2019 report from the World Economic Forum, E-Waste is now the fastest growing waste stream in the world, with an estimated waste stream of 48.5 million tonnes in 2018.

E-waste generating at LPU

According to our analysis, maximum 10 computers become E-waste at LPU, 1000 lights, 15 fans, 4 air-conditioner, nearly 10 coolers and other printers and machines.

Transportation and Labour expenses

According to our analysis, LPU will get nearly 10,000 for selling the recyclable part of e-waste in half a year. It gives nearly 25,000 to labours. They transport these E-waste in nearby state of Haryana facing losses.
